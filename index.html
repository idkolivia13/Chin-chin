Baked chin chin

Ingredients

    • Plain or white flour
    • Liquid milk
    • Nutmeg
    • Baking powder
    • Vanilla flavouring
    • Sugar
    • Butter

Instructions

1.In a bowl,mix in all the dry ingredients.Then add in your butter and mix evenly.

2.Then you add in your vanilla flavouring and liquid milk to make your dough.

3.Depending on the amount you divide your dough into batches.For each batch you will roll it out using a rolling pin,then cut into vertical and horizontal lines.

4.While rolling and cutting each batch,you should heat up your oven.aterwards you place your cut chin chin in your oven tray and bake for 30-40 min on low heat until light brown and crunchy.
